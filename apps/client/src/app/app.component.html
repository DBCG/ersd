This site provides access to the eRSD specifications for electronic Case Reporting (eCR). The eRSD is used to guide triggering and reporting of initial case reports within participating Electronic Health Records. For more information, please visit the EHR triggering documentation for EHR implementers. The site may also contain specifications for other public health reporting programs and pilots.

Registration - If this is your first time visiting the site, please register for an account. Registration is needed so we have contact information to alert you for subsequent routine and emergent eRSD updates.

Access to Content - Once registered, you will receive notices of content updates. You can choose to receive the content through manual download or automated polling of the eRSD server. You will also be able to choose whether you receive the content in XML or JSON formats.
